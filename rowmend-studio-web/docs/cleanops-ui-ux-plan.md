# CleanOps AI Website UI/UX Plan

## 1. Full UI/UX Breakdown

### Homepage structure

1. Hero
   Clear value proposition, two CTAs, trust line, and a practical before/after data visual.
2. Problem section
   Shows the exact admin pain points small businesses feel every week.
3. Services section
   Six focused service cards with audience fit and starting prices.
4. Before/After section
   Visual proof of transformation from messy raw inputs to clean client-ready outputs.
5. How it works
   Four simple steps to reduce friction and make the service feel easy to buy.
6. Pricing
   Three plans that guide visitors toward either one-off tasks or monthly support.
7. Use cases
   Concrete examples so visitors can quickly self-identify.
8. Why choose us
   Trust-building reasons centered on clarity, privacy, and human review.
9. FAQ
   Handles common objections before the visitor leaves the page.
10. Contact
    Two direct channels plus a low-friction intake form.
11. Final CTA
    Repeats the conversion ask with WhatsApp and email options.
12. Footer
    Lightweight site map, contact info, and privacy reassurance.

### Section-by-section layout

#### Hero

- Left column: eyebrow, headline, subheadline, CTA row, trust line, capability chips
- Right column: stacked before/after data panel, task summary card, delivery callout

#### Problem

- Intro copy on top
- Responsive grid of admin pain cards underneath

#### Services

- Section heading
- 3x2 card grid on desktop, single-column stack on mobile
- Each card includes icon, description, ideal customer, and starting price

#### Before/After

- Split comparison card
- One additional summary card showing what the client receives

#### How It Works

- Four numbered cards in a simple linear flow
- Designed to feel calm, scoped, and easy to start

#### Pricing

- Three pricing cards
- Middle plan visually emphasized
- Repeated CTA directly under pricing

#### Use Cases

- Six compact example cards
- Written as real task outcomes, not generic categories

#### Why Choose Us

- Benefit grid with privacy, quality, simplicity, and delivery reassurance

#### FAQ

- Native accordion pattern with short, direct answers

#### Contact

- Left side: direct WhatsApp and email actions
- Right side: short form with essential fields only

### Headlines and copywriting direction

- Tone: practical, calm, trustworthy, direct
- Language: service-first, not startup-first
- Positioning: "AI-assisted and human-reviewed" keeps the offer credible
- Framing: focus on time saved, cleaner outputs, less admin friction

### CTA strategy

- Primary CTA in hero: `Send Your File`
- Secondary CTA in hero: `View Services`
- Pricing CTA repeated after plan comparison
- Mobile sticky CTA stays visible on smaller screens
- Final CTA uses direct channel labels:
  - `Send on WhatsApp`
  - `Email Your Task`

### Visual style

- Light, quiet background with subtle grid and radial accents
- Deep blue for trust and structure
- Emerald for positive action and "clean output" signaling
- Soft white cards, rounded corners, thin borders, and restrained shadows
- Visual metaphors: spreadsheets, rows, tags, delivery summaries, workflow cards

### Color palette

- Canvas: `#f5f7f2`
- Surface: `#ffffff`
- Surface muted: `#edf2ed`
- Ink: `#102028`
- Text muted: `#5d6a72`
- Line: `#d7e0db`
- Brand: `#18394c`
- Brand strong: `#102b3a`
- Accent: `#2f8a73`
- Accent soft: `#dbefe8`
- Warm highlight: `#f0e7d4`

### Typography direction

- Primary font: Geist Sans for clean, strong, modern readability
- Monospace accent: Geist Mono for prices, tags, and structured data labels
- Headline style: large, compact line height, strong weight
- Body style: 16px minimum on mobile, comfortable line height

### Mobile-first layout

- Single-column stacking everywhere by default
- Sticky bottom CTA on mobile
- Compact navigation with only critical links visible
- Pricing, service cards, and FAQs optimized for thumb scrolling

### Pricing structure

- Starter Task: from `$15 / INR 999`
- Standard Task: from `$39 / INR 2,999`
- Monthly Support: from `$199 / INR 14,999`
- Clarifying note:
  `Final price depends on file size, deadline, and complexity.`

### Trust-building elements

- Human-reviewed output
- Privacy-conscious process
- Clear scope confirmation before work starts
- No new software required
- Easy delivery through WhatsApp or email
- Practical examples instead of vague AI claims

### Suggested animations and interactions

- Subtle floating motion on the hero data visual
- Gentle card lift on hover for desktop
- Smooth in-page anchor scrolling
- FAQ expansion with native details/summary behavior
- Motion reduced automatically for users who prefer less animation

### Responsive behavior

- 1 column on small screens
- 2 columns for hero, contact, and comparison sections on large screens
- 3-column service and pricing layouts on desktop
- Sticky CTA hidden on desktop and visible on mobile

### Accessibility considerations

- Skip link for keyboard users
- Visible focus styles
- 16px body text minimum on mobile
- High-contrast text and button states
- Native form labels and helper text
- Native details/summary for FAQ accessibility
- Reduced motion support

### Conversion flow

1. Visitor understands the offer in the hero
2. Visitor sees relevant service or use case
3. Visitor gains trust through pricing, process, and FAQ
4. Visitor clicks WhatsApp, email, or fills the short intake form

## 2. Polished Website Copy

### Hero

- Eyebrow: `AI-assisted back-office support`
- Headline: `Messy files in. Clean business-ready work out.`
- Subheadline:
  `CleanOps AI helps small teams clean spreadsheets, organize business data, transcribe recordings, summarize documents, and handle repetitive admin work with fast AI-assisted, human-reviewed delivery.`
- Trust line:
  `Spreadsheet cleanup, transcription, summaries, and admin automation for small teams.`

### Problem section

- Heading: `Admin work gets expensive when the data is messy.`
- Body:
  `Duplicate contacts, broken CSVs, scattered notes, long recordings, and endless copy-paste work slow down teams that should be spending time on clients, sales, and delivery.`

### Services

- Spreadsheet Cleanup
  `Fix formatting, clean headers, standardize columns, remove duplicates, and prepare sheets for real use.`
- Lead Research
  `Build clean lead sheets with the right fields, categories, and outreach-ready structure.`
- Transcription and Summaries
  `Turn meetings, podcasts, calls, or interviews into readable transcripts and useful summaries.`
- CRM Cleanup
  `Organize exports, deduplicate contacts, and make old CRM data usable again.`
- Document Summaries
  `Condense long reports, proposals, SOPs, or research files into clear action-ready notes.`
- Workflow Automation
  `Set up simple AI-assisted admin workflows for recurring repetitive tasks.`

### Before/After section

- Heading: `From messy raw data to client-ready output`
- Supporting copy:
  `We do not just process files. We organize them so they are easier to use, easier to share, and easier to trust.`

### How It Works

- Step 1: `Send your file or task`
- Step 2: `We review it and confirm scope`
- Step 3: `We clean, organize, or summarize`
- Step 4: `You receive the final output`

### Pricing

- Starter Task
  `Best for quick cleanup jobs, short summaries, and small admin tasks.`
- Standard Task
  `Best for larger spreadsheets, transcriptions, structured research, or CRM cleanup.`
- Monthly Support
  `Best for recurring back-office help across spreadsheets, admin, summaries, and workflow support.`

### Final CTA

- Heading: `Have a messy file or admin task? Send it today.`
- Supporting copy:
  `Share the task, get a clear scope, and receive clean usable output without turning it into a bigger project than it needs to be.`

## 3. Component/Page Structure

```text
src/
  app/
    _components/
      landing-page.tsx
    _content/
      site-content.ts
    globals.css
    layout.tsx
    page.tsx
docs/
  cleanops-ui-ux-plan.md
```

### Component roles

- `page.tsx`
  Thin route entry point.
- `landing-page.tsx`
  Main homepage composition and reusable section components.
- `site-content.ts`
  Centralized copy, pricing, FAQs, service cards, and navigation labels.
- `globals.css`
  Design tokens, background treatment, motion, and focus styling.

### Contact

- Keep one strong CTA visible near the bottom
