# RowMend Studio Website UI/UX Plan

## 1. Full UI/UX Breakdown

### Homepage structure

1. Hero
   Clear value proposition, two CTAs, audience fit, and a practical before/after delivery visual.
2. Trust strip
   Shows the most believable proof points without fake logos or testimonials.
3. Problem section
   Frames the admin and data issues that slow small teams down.
4. Services section
   Six focused services with practical descriptions and starting prices.
5. Before/After section
   Demonstrates how raw exports turn into organized deliverables.
6. How it works
   Four-step process that reduces buying friction.
7. Use cases
   Concrete examples that help visitors recognize their own task.
8. Pricing
   Three simple offers for one-off tasks and recurring support.
9. Why choose RowMend Studio
   Trust-building reasons centered on review, clarity, and ease.
10. FAQ
    Handles common objections before the visitor leaves the page.
11. Contact and final CTA
    Direct channels plus a short form for scope intake.
12. Footer
    Lightweight brand summary, services, and contact details.

### Visual direction

- Minimal and sophisticated
- Practical service-business feel, not startup hype
- Light background with subtle tonal depth
- Strong typography and calm spacing
- Refined cards, borders, and restrained shadows
- Abstract data and handoff visuals instead of AI clichés

### Core copy direction

- Direct and human
- Confident without exaggeration
- Outcome-focused rather than feature-heavy
- Service-first language throughout

### Trust signals

- Human-reviewed output
- Scope confirmation before work starts
- Delivery notes and handoff summary
- Common business file types supported
- Monthly support available for recurring work

### Mobile behavior

- Single-column stacking by default
- Comfortable spacing and button sizes
- Sticky mobile CTA
- Hero visual simplified into a clear vertical story
